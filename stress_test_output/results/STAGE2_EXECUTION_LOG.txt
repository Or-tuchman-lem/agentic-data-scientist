KEY DISCOVERIES:
================================================================

1. BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY:
   Best variant (70_100_50000) shows 13.74% improvement over production
   = $1.35M annual revenue impact (100k quotes/year assumption)

2. STATISTICAL REALITY:
   Despite meaningful business impact, no traditional statistical significance
   due to study being underpowered (need 6,300/variant, have 2,100/variant)

3. PARAMETER INSIGHTS:
   - Coinsurance: 70% significantly outperforms 80%/90% (p=0.0056)
   - Deductible: $250 slight edge but not significant
   - Coverage: $20k performs best but not significant

4. SEGMENT OPPORTUNITIES:
   - High income: +35.5% potential improvement
   - Senior pets: Production severely underperforms (0.71% conv)
   - Adult pets: Highest sales potential overall

5. TEMPORAL STABILITY:
   Top variants maintain rankings across 9 months (72% stability)
