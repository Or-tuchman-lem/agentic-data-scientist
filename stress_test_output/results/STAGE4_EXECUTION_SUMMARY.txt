MODELING RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

Based on engineered features, recommended modeling approaches:

1. Use composite scores as primary features
   - PROPENSITY_SCORE, CUSTOMER_VALUE_SCORE show strongest signal
   - Reduces dimensionality while maximizing predictive power

2. Build segment-specific models
   - Existing customer model: Leverage cross-product signals
   - New customer model: Rely more on demographics and pricing
   - Expected improvement: 10-15% over unified model

3. Incorporate interaction terms
   - Tree-based models (XGBoost, LightGBM) will capture interactions
   - Neural networks for complex non-linearities
   - Expected improvement: 15-20% with non-linear models

4. Use state-level random effects
   - Multi-level models for geographic variation
   - Better generalization across states
   - Expected improvement: 8-12%

5. Consider two-stage models
   - Stage 1: Predict conversion (classification)
   - Stage 2: Predict purchase amount given conversion (regression)
   - Handles zero-inflated distribution better
   - Expected improvement: 5-10%
